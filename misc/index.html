books i've been reading 📓

recently, i've been on a non-fiction kick, and have been reading a handful of books related to digital media, user experience and technology as a whole. this list contains all the books i've read related to the field!

  • Universal Methods of Design by Bella Martin & Bruce Hannington
  • The User Experience Team of One by Leah Buley
  • Interviewing Users by Steve Portigal
  • Digital Minimalism by Cal Newport
  • The Design of Everyday Things by Don Norman (IN PROGRESS)
  • Understanding Industrial Design by Simon King & Kuen Chang (IN PROGRESS)

out of these books, i was graciously lended the first three from one of my supervisors over my first work term after expressing interest in UX, while the others i've picked up for myself out of further interest.
